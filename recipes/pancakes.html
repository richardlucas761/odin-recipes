Fluffy pancakes with blueberry sauce recipe

Fluffy blueberry pancakes with a spiced blueberry sauce for one.

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 60ml/2¼fl oz milk
  • ½ punnet blueberries
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 25g/1oz butter

Blueberry sauce

  • 4 tbsp red wine
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves
  • pinch mixed spice
  • ½ punnet blueberries

Steps

  1. Place in a bowl the self-raising flour, baking powder, eggs and milk. Mix together to form a thick batter.
  2. Add the icing sugar and blueberries.
  3. Melt the butter in a frying pan and ladle in the blueberry pancake mix a ladleful at a time. Cook the pancakes until golden brown, on both sides.
  4. Stack the blueberry pancakes and leave to one side.
  5. For the blueberry sauce, in a pan add the red wine with the sugar, cloves, mixed spice and the blueberries.
  6. Simmer for four minutes, and take off the heat.
  7. Put the sauce through a sieve and remove the cloves.
  8. To serve, place the stack of pancakes in the middle of the plate and drizzle with the blueberry sauce.
